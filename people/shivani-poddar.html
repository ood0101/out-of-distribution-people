Background

Moved to the US for graduate studies in 2016. CMU Language Technologies Institute, MLT (Master of Language Technologies), 2018–2020, advised by Carolyn Rosé. The first CMU student funded by the Amazon Alexa Prize. Research focus: natural language generation, RL for chatbots, multimodal machine learning. Selected as part of the CMU Magnus team for the inaugural Alexa Prize SocialBot Grand Challenge (2016–17). Magnus was one of 12 funded teams; the final 2017 prize went to University of Washington’s Sounding Board. Magnus did not place top-3 but the system was deployed to tens of thousands of users via Alexa.

Joined Facebook (now Meta) ~2020 as an AR/VR ML engineer on the Facebook Assistant team. Promoted to ML Tech Lead for the Workplace Meeting Assistant team. Built and launched the foundational ML/AI stack for Facebook Portal. Co-author on the SIMMC line of multimodal assistant research and two granted US patents on multimodal assistant systems. Speaker at RE•WORK AI Assistant Summit (2020), ML Conf EU (2020), Chatbot Summit.

Moved to Google DeepMind ~2022–23 as Sr. Engineering Lead, Applied Research. Worked on frontier model launches (likely Gemini era, given timing). Featured speaker at Google I/O 2023. Listed on the official Google Research people directory (research.google/people/108134).
