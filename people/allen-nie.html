Research & Key Work

Problems & Research Areas

  • Reinforcement learning (offline RL, in-context RL, causality)
  • NLP and language models
  • Clinical decision making with AI
  • AI for education
  • EHR foundation models (EHRSHOT, CLMBR-T-base)
  • LLM agents and optimization

Key Work

EHRSHOT: EHR benchmark for few-shot evaluation of foundation models (released clinical foundation model). EVOLvE: Evaluating and Optimizing LLMs for Exploration (ICML 2025). DeepTag & VetTag (Nature Digital Medicine). DisSent (ACL 2019). OPERA (NeurIPS 2024). Best Paper ICML 2025 EXAIT.

Current Work

Research Scientist at Google DeepMind working on reinforcement learning.

◆ Alpha Insight

One of the most prolific researchers in the cohort (12K+ citations). The EHRSHOT work is becoming a standard clinical AI benchmark. His unique span across RL, NLP, clinical AI, and education means he can identify cross-domain opportunities others miss. The move to DeepMind RL team suggests focus on agentic systems.
