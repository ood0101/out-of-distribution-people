Research & Publications

CLAMP: Contrastive Language And Molecule Pre-training Network

“CLAMP: A Contrastive Language And Molecule Pre-training Network”
NeurIPS 2023 · Independent researcher · Emergent Ventures funded · arXiv:2311.07617

Proposes language-to-material generation (inverting the standard material-to-material approach). Uses a web scraper to collect crystal-text pairs from open-source research papers, then trains a contrastive model with a convolutional graph neural network encoder and a language encoder. ~82% zero-shot classification accuracy; ~75% for photocatalyst prediction with minimal data. Cross-applicable to any reaction describable via text.

CarbNN: AI for Carbon Capture

“CarbNN: A Novel Active Transfer Learning Neural Network To Build De Novo Metal Organic Frameworks for Carbon Capture”
AAAI 2023 (37th Conference) · Poster · Davidson Fellow ($25K) · arXiv:2311.16158

Designed a neural network that identified more energy- and cost-efficient MOFs for pulling hydrocarbons from the air. Uses electrocatalysis to split trapped CO&sub2; into sellable carbon monoxide and oxygen — turning carbon capture from a cost center into a revenue source. One of only 21 students nationally recognized as a 2023 Davidson Fellow.
