Research & Key Work

Problems & Research Areas

  • Scaling laws for reinforcement learning
  • Compute-optimal scaling for value-based RL
  • Building systems that efficiently learn general skills

Key Work

Scaling laws for value-based RL (ICML 2025). Compute-optimal scaling for value-based RL (NeurIPS 2025). Collaborators include Sergey Levine, Aviral Kumar, Pieter Abbeel.

Current Work

Undergrad at UC Berkeley doing RL scaling laws research at RAIL/BAIR, advised by Sergey Levine and Aviral Kumar.

◆ Alpha Insight

Working on the equivalent of Chinchilla scaling laws but for RL -- understanding compute-optimal training for RL agents. This is foundational work that will shape how the industry trains agentic AI systems.
