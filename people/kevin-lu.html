◆ Alpha Insight

Kevin's trajectory from Decision Transformer (reframing RL as sequence modeling) to leading small model training at OpenAI to Thinking Machines reveals a consistent thesis: the magic is in the data and training recipe, not the architecture. His view that RL cannot yet achieve GPT-1-to-GPT-4 level jumps suggests Thinking Machines is betting on pre-training innovation.
