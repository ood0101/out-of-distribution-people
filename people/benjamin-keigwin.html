Tilde Research — the Company

Tilde Research (tilderesearch.com) is a Palo Alto "moonshot AI lab advancing mechanistic interpretability, new architectures, and pretraining science," founded 2024, ~6 people. It raised an $8M seed led by Khosla Ventures (Soma Capital also in). Co-founders: Benjamin Keigwin (CEO) and Dhruv Pai (Chief Scientist; Stanford STAIR lab, learning dynamics).

Shipped technical artifacts (the reason this is a credible lab, not a thesis deck):

  • Sieve — "SAEs Beat Baselines on a Real-World Task," a code-generation case study showing sparse autoencoders outperforming baselines on an applied task, with open weights/code (tilde-research/sieve). Applied interpretability with a measurable win.
  • Telescope — their interpreter-model / model-control product line.
  • MoMoE / SonicMoE — mixture-of-experts architecture + systems work; SonicMoE accepted at ICLR 2026. Signals the lab does serious pretraining/systems work, not only SAE probing.
