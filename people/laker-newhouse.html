◆ Alpha Insight

Muon is not just another optimizer — it's derived from first principles (steepest descent under operator norm), which means it has theoretical backing that Adam lacks. Laker's framework for understanding all optimizers as 'steepest descent under a norm' could generate a whole family of better optimizers for different architectures.
