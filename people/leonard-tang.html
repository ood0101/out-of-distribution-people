◆ Alpha Insight

Haize Labs occupies a unique position: they are hired by the very labs (Anthropic, etc.) building frontier models to break those models. The insight is that safety is a scaling problem — you can't manually red-team models fast enough, so you need ML systems to adversarially attack other ML systems. This creates a durable moat as models get more capable.
