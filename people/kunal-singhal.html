What He’s Building: Effective AI

Effective AI (effectiveailabs.com) is an AI agent platform for insurance professionals handling pricing, market entry, and business growth. Founded 2025, SF-based, 19 employees, backed by Lightspeed and Valor.

Product

  • Market intelligence: Ask about any market, get cited briefs — which carriers write it, how they price it, regulatory objections, recent changes. Every answer linked to sources and filings
  • Competitive pricing: Turns public rate filings into working rating algorithms. Agents run simulations breaking down where you win/lose and why, down to individual rating factors
  • Interactive analysis: Follow-up in plain language, no batch jobs. Conversations with agents that re-slice analysis in real time
  • Formal verification: AI agents with formal verification and multi-agent coordination — output is auditable, traceable deliverables, not summaries
  • Coverage: Personal auto, homeowners, commercial lines — markets that have never had competitive pricing tools

Target Users

Actuaries, underwriters, adjusters, agents, and claims professionals. The output is finished work — a completed rater, a cited exhibit, a competitive analysis formatted to regulatory standards. Not a copilot; a replacement for the spreadsheet/PDF workflow.
