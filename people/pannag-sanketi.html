The Spike — With Evidence

Three distinct, verifiable peaks — rare to find all three in one person:

1. Founder of Open X-Embodiment / RT-X — organizational leadership at field scale

Open X-Embodiment (Oct 2023, arXiv 2310.08864, 1,646 Scholar cites) pooled robot-trajectory data from 21+ institutions into a single dataset — 500+ skills, 150,000+ tasks, 22 robot embodiments, 1M+ episodes — and trained the RT-X generalist models on it. The paper carries 170+ authors; Sanketi is credited as a founder / co-lead of the collaboration (his LinkedIn and ContactOut both list “Lead of Open X-Embodiment collaboration”). In his own framing with co-lead Quan Vuong: “Just as ImageNet propelled computer vision research, we believe Open X-Embodiment can do the same to advance robotics.” Convening 21 competing labs around shared data is not a research-IC act — it is founder-grade coalition-building.

2. RT-2 / the VLA lineage — technical depth in the dominant robot-learning paradigm

He is a co-author on RT-2: Vision-Language-Action Models (2023, 3,713 cites — his single most-cited paper), the work that established that web-scale VLM knowledge transfers to robot control. His Scholar profile is a who's-who of the modern robot-foundation-model canon: RT-1 (2,591), OpenVLA (2,592), Octo (1,485), DROID (732), Gemini Robotics (344, 2025). This is the exact technical surface — VLAs trained on pooled embodiment data — that every well-funded humanoid/general-robotics company is now racing on.

3. Table Tennis + agility — dynamic whole-body control, distinct from manipulation

He led the DeepMind robot table tennis program — Achieving Human Level Competitive Robot Table Tennis (arXiv 2408.03906, Aug 2024): the first learned robot agent to reach amateur human-level in competitive play, with a 45% win rate across human opponents of varying skill, a hierarchical low-level-skill / high-level-controller architecture, and zero-shot sim-to-real with real-time opponent adaptation. This is dynamic, high-frequency whole-body control — a different and harder regime than the quasi-static manipulation most VLA work targets. The breadth (data + VLA + agility + on-device ML) is the signal: he is not a one-trick researcher.
