The Spike — With Evidence

Video Arena: Thijs built Video Arena from scratch, starting as a Discord bot experiment in summer 2025 and launching on web in January 2026 at lmarena.ai/video. It is the largest preference evaluation platform for generative video models, with 200,000+ human preference votes across 15 frontier models including Google Veo 3, Sora 2, Seedance v1.5 Pro, Kling-2.6-pro, WAN-2.5, and Hailuo-2.3. Video Arena provides both Text-to-Video and Image-to-Video leaderboards. Anastasios Angelopoulos (Arena co-founder, Berkeley professor) publicly praised the work: “Video Arena is kind of goated.”

Truth or Dare Bot: Built at ~age 15. 500,000 monthly active users. Deployed across 1.6 million Discord communities (~16 million total users). 500 people request questions every minute at any given time. ~4,000 questions across 5 categories (Truth, Dare, Would You Rather, Never Have I Ever, Paranoia). The backend handles 12 req/s at peak on just 200MB RAM, built with Fastify in TypeScript. Open-source interaction handler on GitHub (github.com/tandpfun/truth-or-dare).

$100K Bug Bounty: Earned a $100,000 payout for discovering a major security vulnerability (company redacted on his portfolio). This is in the top tier of bug bounty payouts globally — most programs cap at $10K–$50K.
