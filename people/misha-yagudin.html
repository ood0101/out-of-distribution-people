◆ Index Rationale — T3 (network node, off-core-thesis)

Yagudin is genuinely elite at his thing — co-founder/leader of Samotsvety, which holds a verifiable top-tier superforecasting record (1st in INFER/CSET-Foretell 2021 at ~2× the 2nd-best team; “1st–4th all-time” INFER places; Yagudin personally 3rd all-time). He also co-founded Sage (forecasting tools / Quantified Intuitions) and is a long-standing AI-risk forecaster (Samotsvety AI-risk forecasts, AGI timelines).

But this is off the core thesis. His spike is forecasting accuracy, full stop — no technical/ML building (his EA bio explicitly frames it as “forecasting and AI risk assessment, not technical ML/AI development”; his only ML-paper credit is as a co-author on Leech’s meta-science evals critique). And there’s no foundable inflection of his own — Samotsvety/Sage/Arb are forecasting collectives/consultancies, and OLO DIN is Galatenko’s pre-product vehicle that he co-operates. Not a PASS only because the OLO DIN operating role + the elite-forecaster credibility make him a legitimate node to keep on file; but there’s no path to T1/T2 absent him personally building something technical. His real value to us is the network.
