◆ Investment Thesis — T0 (Active Pursuit)

Corrado Pezzato is a clean inception-stage founding moment in a genuinely out-of-distribution corner of robotics. His own LinkedIn headline reads, verbatim, “Co-Founder and Head of Robotics at The Blanket” — corroborated by his Google Scholar affiliation flipping from VERSES Research Lab to The Blanket. The Blanket is a stealth, pre-funding active-inference robotics startup (“Robots that think before they act”; product auto·motion — zero-programming robot deployment, operational in hours, online self-calibration, human-in-the-loop when uncertain) that spun out of the VERSES robotics team in 2026. The company’s own site openly solicits investors. This is exactly the pre-company / just-co-founded moment the fund targets.

The spike is the tightest possible fit between the founder’s research and the product. He is one of perhaps a dozen people globally credible to commercialize this.
