Research & Key Work

Problems & Research Areas

  • Robot learning and embodied AI
  • Drone-based wildfire detection
  • Aptamer-based electrochemical sensing for stroke diagnosis
  • Low-cost robotics arms
  • AI for assistive robotics
  • Hardware CAD version control

Key Work

Research at BAIR on robot learning algorithms. Founded nonprofit connecting students to high-impact tech projects in developing countries (Chile, Guatemala, Philippines, Kenya, Uganda). Built CAD version control platform at Entrepreneur First.

Companies Founded

  • 501(c)(3) AI for Assistive Robotics nonprofit
  • Hardware CAD platform (Entrepreneur First)

Current Work

Senior EECS at Berkeley focused on creating algorithms that enable robots to learn by experiencing the world. Researcher at BAIR.

◆ Alpha Insight

Bridging the gap between software AI and physical hardware with both a nonprofit (assistive robotics) and a startup (CAD version control) angle. The hardware CAD version control idea addresses a massive unmet need in hardware engineering workflows.
