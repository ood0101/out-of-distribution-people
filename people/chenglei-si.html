◆ Alpha Insight

Chenglei's research idea generation study is one of the most discussed AI papers of 2024-2025. The finding that LLM ideas are more novel BUT score worse after execution reveals a crucial insight: ideation and execution are fundamentally different capabilities. This has profound implications for how AI will transform R&D — AI may be best as an 'idea generator' paired with human executors, not as an end-to-end researcher.
