Byline verbatim (verified against arXiv’s own citation metadata, the project page, and the repo): Ninad Daithankar* · Alexi Gladstone* · Yann LeCun · Heng Ji (* = equal contribution; Daithankar / Gladstone / Ji = UIUC, LeCun = NYU).

  • What it does (verified quote): TDV “jointly trains an image encoder and a motion encoder so that the current frame’s representation plus the encoded motion equals the next frame’s representation.” The pitch: a single causal assumption (“the past predicts the future”) replaces augmentations / masking / cropping. Treat as a claim, not a result, until NeurIPS review or independent reproduction.
