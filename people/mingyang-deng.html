Research & Key Work

Problems & Research Areas

  • Generative models / diffusion models
  • Autoregressive image generation
  • Inference scaling for math olympiad problem solving
  • Theoretical computer science (subset sum, knapsack, graph algorithms)
  • Sampling methods for generative processes

Key Work

Autoregressive Image Generation without Vector Quantization (NeurIPS 2024 Spotlight, with Kaiming He). Restart Sampling for Improving Generative Processes (NeurIPS 2023). Multiple SODA/ICALP papers on approximation algorithms. Contributed to IMO silver-medal AI system.

Current Work

PhD Candidate at MIT CSAIL advised by Kaiming He and formerly Tommi Jaakkola. Working on generative models and inference scaling.

◆ Alpha Insight

Extremely rare dual expertise in both theoretical computer science (SODA, ICALP) and cutting-edge ML (NeurIPS orals/spotlights with Kaiming He). The IMO silver-medal AI work suggests deep understanding of inference-time compute scaling for reasoning.
