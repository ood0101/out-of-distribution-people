◆ Alpha Insight

Kevin's NeurIPS Best Paper proved the most contrarian result in recent RL: that depth scaling (up to 1024 layers) unlocks massive gains in self-supervised RL, while the entire field has been using 2-5 layer networks for decades. This is the 'scaling hypothesis' hitting RL — the same insight that transformed NLP and vision. At OpenAI, he's presumably applying this scaling intuition to their RL post-training pipelines, which is the secret sauce behind o1/o3.
