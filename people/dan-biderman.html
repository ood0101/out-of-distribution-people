◆ Alpha Insight

Dan's Minions work may define how LLMs are actually deployed: not as monolithic cloud models, but as swarms of cheap local models guided by expensive cloud models. The 5.7x cost reduction at 97.9% performance recovery is exactly the economics that makes AI deployment viable at scale.
