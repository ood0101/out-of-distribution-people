# Augustus Odena

![](https://www.augustusodena.com/assets/purple.jpg)

I'm an AI researcher and entrepreneur.


I co-founded [Adept](https://www.adept.ai/), where I was responsible for the research program.
We built the first [Computer Use Agents](https://www.adept.ai/blog/act-1) and the [Fuyu](https://www.adept.ai/blog/fuyu-8b) [family](https://www.adept.ai/blog/adept-fuyu-heavy) of multi-modal models.
We exited to Amazon in 2024. From June through November of 2025, I was a Research Scientist in TBD Labs at Meta. I've recently started something new.


Along with my Adept co-founder [Max Nye](https://maxwellnye.com/), I discovered/invented the notion of test-time-compute for Transformers.
Our [Scratchpad Technique](https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.00114) (popularly known as chain-of-thought) is the basis for all [modern](https://openai.com/o1/) [reasoning](https://docs.anthropic.com/en/docs/build-with-claude/prompt-engineering/chain-of-thought) [systems](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/thinking).
I also hold the [patent](https://patents.google.com/patent/US20230244938A1/en) for Chain-of-Thought prompting.


Prior to Adept, I was at Google Brain for ~5 years.
In addition to the Scratchpad work, I did a lot of research on Program Synthesis.
I led Brain's work on [Program Synthesis with Large Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2108.07732)
and co-created Google Sheet's [SmartFill Program Synthesizer](https://workspaceupdates.googleblog.com/2020/10/smart-fill-google-sheets-automate-data-entry.html),


Before I worked on program synthesis, I worked on generative modeling.
Along with collaborators, I did a lot of the early work on scaling up
[Generative Adversarial Networks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generative_adversarial_network).
That includes [the first GAN to generate real ImageNet samples](https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.09585),
[the paper that made Self-Attention work in GANs](https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.08318), and
[work that diagnosed and fixed the frequency artifacts that plagued early synthesis models](https://distill.pub/2016/deconv-checkerboard/).


I have also worked on [Semi-Supervised Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/1804.09170)
and other, miscellaneous topics, such as
[Coverage-Guided Fuzzing of Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1807.10875).


I came to Brain by being in the first batch of [Google Brain Residents](https://ai.google/research/join-us/ai-residency/),
before which I worked at a startup called [Nervana Systems](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nervana_Systems).
Before all of that, I was a trader: I worked at [Five Rings Capital](https://fiverings.com/)
and [Millennium](https://www.mlp.com/home/).
Before I was a trader, I was a Math major at
[Columbia University](https://www.columbia.edu/).
