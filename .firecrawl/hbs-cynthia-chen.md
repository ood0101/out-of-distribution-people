## Formative experience at the intersection of technology and business:

As a software intern at Scale AI, I had the unique opportunity to help build Scale’s Enterprise Generative AI Platform, which enables enterprises to build LLM applications on their own data. Over the course of a summer, I shipped end-to-end APIs for data integration, drove efforts to improve observability in our backend processes, and led the creation of our API documentation, all while working in a small team of engineers to drive our ambitious vision forward.

Being at Scale gave me a front-row seat into both the growing pains and successes that come with startups, redefining the way I think about scaling a new product. I grew immensely from the experience and am incredibly grateful to have had the chance to learn from those I worked with.
