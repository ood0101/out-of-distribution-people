Assistant Professor

[Cornell CS](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/)

I am an Assistant Professor at [Cornell CS](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/) and ML Researcher at [Aurora](https://aurora.tech/aurora-driver).

I work on interactive AI agents that self-align through few-shot interactions with humans and their environment.

My research focuses on reinforcement learning (RLHF), imitation learning (IRL), and foundation models for planning, robotics, and code generation.

I also lead the [PoRTaL](https://portal.cs.cornell.edu/) group. We build everyday robots for everyday users!
