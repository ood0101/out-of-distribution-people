# Arda raises $70M at $700M valuation to automate Western manufacturing with AI software

by [Paul Drecksler](https://www.shopifreaks.com/author/paul-drecksler/ "Posts by Paul Drecksler") \| Mar 5, 2026 \| [E-commerce News](https://www.shopifreaks.com/category/ecommerce-news/)

![black and white street sign](https://www.shopifreaks.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/cevj8lpbjsc.jpg)

Arda, an AI software startup co-founded by former OpenAI research chief Bob McGrew, raised $70 million at a $700 million valuation. The company developed a platform featuring a video model to train robots and coordinate autonomous factory operations. The funding round, co-led by Founders Fund and Accel, supported a goal to make Western manufacturing cost-effective and reduce supply chain reliance on China.
