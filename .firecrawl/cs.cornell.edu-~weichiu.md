## About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at [Cornell University](http://cornell.edu/).

My research lies at the intersection of 3D/4D computer vision and robotics. I am interested in building AI systems that can **understand**, **reconstruct**, and **re-simulate** our dynamic world, and leverage these capabilities to enable more robust autonomous systems or advance entertainment applications.



Prior to joining Cornell, I was a Young Investigator/Postdoc at [AI2](https://allenai.org/)/ [University of Washington](https://www.washington.edu/).
I received my Ph.D. from [MIT](http://web.mit.edu/), where I worked with [Antonio Torralba](http://web.mit.edu/torralba/www/)
(aka [the Great Torralba](http://bit.ly/the-great-torralba)) and [Raquel Urtasun](http://www.cs.toronto.edu/~urtasun/).
Previously, I was a Senior Research Scientist at [Uber ATG R&D](https://eng.uber.com/tag/uber-atg-toronto/) and [Waabi](https://waabi.ai/) working on self-driving vehicles. I completed my M.S. in Robotics at [Carnegie Mellon University (CMU)](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/), where I was advised by [Kris M. Kitani](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~kkitani/).

**Prospective students:** I am always looking for motivated and talented students! If you are interested in collaborating or joining my group as a PhD/MS/Undergrad student or intern, please read [this](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/~weichiu/prospective.html).
