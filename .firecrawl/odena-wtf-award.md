# Augustus Odena Earns WTF Innovators Award

SALT LAKE CITY, June 28, 2023 /PRNewswire/ -- QuHarrison Terry [presents Augustus Odena, Co-founder of Adept](https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&l=en&o=3907674-1&h=310842279&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.everydays.wtf%2Faugustus-odena%2F&a=presents+Augustus+Odena%2C+Co-founder+of+Adept), with the WTF Innovators Award for his remarkable research into AI for image synthesis and language model contributions at Google Brain which led to features like SmartFill.

[The WTF Innovators Award](https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&l=en&o=3907674-1&h=1000282670&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.everydays.wtf%2F34-ai-innovators%2F&a=The+WTF+Innovators+Award) recognizes excellence at the precipice of societal change, with the inaugural class focusing on AI innovators. As a memento, each of the 34 awardees are gifted a featured song by QuHarrison Terry and Genesis Renji. We present "Odena", produced by [Nimso](https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&l=en&o=3907674-1&h=2541437596&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.instagram.com%2Fnimsobeats%2F&a=Nimso), to Augustus Odena.

Listen to "Odena": [https://qt.lnk.to/OdenaPr](https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&l=en&o=3907674-1&h=2357597482&u=https%3A%2F%2Fqt.lnk.to%2FOdenaPr&a=https%3A%2F%2Fqt.lnk.to%2FOdenaPr)

[Adept](https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&l=en&o=3907674-1&h=950757703&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.adept.ai%2F&a=Adept) is building a machine learning model that can interact with everything on your computer and will fundamentally change how we operate digitally. The unreleased product will be a general intelligence system or "AI Teammate" that augments and supercharges actions on any website or software tool.

In March 2023, Adept raised a $350M Series B round led by General Catalyst and Spark Capital.

Adept's first step toward this future is their foundation model, ACT-1, a large-scale Transformer for actions that has been trained to click, type, and scroll. The multimodal model observes what is displayed on-screen, comprehends a user's request from natural language, and then executes the task.

"Augustus Odena has an impressive track record of advancing AI in the most relevant categories today. He's been a core researcher to many important revelations for language models and GANs. And he also has a keen sense for devising AI systems that are human-centered and useful to knowledge workers, which is why he should be on everyone's radar," said [QuHarrison Terry](https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&l=en&o=3907674-1&h=2153328912&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.linkedin.com%2Fin%2Fquharrison-terry-728b3a89%2F&a=QuHarrison+Terry).

Prior to co-founding Adept, Augustus Odena was a Research Scientist at Google Brain. Augustus led Google Brain's work on [Program Synthesis with Large Language Models](https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&l=en&o=3907674-1&h=345935837&u=https%3A%2F%2Farxiv.org%2Fabs%2F2108.07732&a=Program+Synthesis+with+Large+Language+Models), training LLMs to write Python code from short natural language descriptions. He co-created the SmartFill Program Synthesizer which is used today across Google Sheets. And he jointly invented the [Scratchpad Technique](https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&l=en&o=3907674-1&h=1611322468&u=https%3A%2F%2Farxiv.org%2Fabs%2F2112.00114&a=Scratchpad+Technique) for getting Transformers to perform multi-step reasoning.

Additionally, Augustus' [research papers](https://c212.net/c/link/?t=0&l=en&o=3907674-1&h=3204372706&u=https%3A%2F%2Fscholar.google.com%2Fcitations%3Fuser%3DEHQHNdEAAAAJ%26hl%3Den&a=research+papers) on Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) for image synthesis have been cited over 8,000 times and pioneered techniques used across the graphics industry.
