About Decagon

## The way we see it, every customer everywhere deserves experiences that are personalized, proactive, and always available. The standard should be that if you’re a customer of something, it should feel like you’re the only one — as if you have your own AI concierge. Every business deserves the technology to make this possible.

## Our platform enables leading enterprises like Avis Budget Group, Chime, Oura Health, 1-800-FLOWERS.COM, and Hunter Douglas to deploy AI agents that deliver deeply satisfying, tailored experiences across voice, chat, email, SMS, and every other customer channel.

## We’re building a future where customer experiences are being redefined from support tickets and hold music to faster resolutions, richer conversations, and deeper relationships. We’re proud to be backed by world-class investors who share that vision, including a16z, Accel, Bain Capital Ventures, Coatue, and Index Ventures, along with many others.
