PhD Student

[Cornell University](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/)

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Cornell University (expected graduation in May 2026), advised by [Prof. Sanjiban Choudhury](https://www.sanjibanchoudhury.com/) and [Prof. Wei-Chiu Ma](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/~weichiu/). Currently, I am a visiting researcher working with [Prof. Jeannette Bohg](https://web.stanford.edu/~bohg/) and [Prof. C. Karen Liu](https://profiles.stanford.edu/c-karen-liu) at Stanford University.

My research goal is to build real-world robot systems that match human capabilities and are able to seamlessly collaborate with human partners. Towards this goal, I work on learning algorithms for robot decision making. I am especially interested in transfer learning from human videos and simulation.

In the past, I completed my undergraduate studies at IIT Kharagpur and have interned at [Cruise](https://www.getcruise.com/) and [Microsoft Research India](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/lab/microsoft-research-india/).
