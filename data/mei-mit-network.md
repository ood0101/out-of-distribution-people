## 5. MIT Kavli Institute -- Astrophysics to AI Pipeline?

The MIT Kavli Institute for Astrophysics and Space Research is:
- One of MIT's most prestigious research institutes (founded 1963)
- Focuses on cosmology, exoplanets, dark matter, compact objects, high-energy astrophysics, gravitational radiation
- Admits ~10 new astro grad students/year
- Undergrads can do research through direct contact with faculty

**AI/ML at Kavli:** No direct AI/ML research found at Kavli itself. However, astrophysics increasingly uses ML for data analysis (TESS mission data, spectral analysis, etc.). The quantitative rigor of astrophysics research is directly transferable to AI/ML intuition.

**What "Lecture Assistant" means:** This is likely a UROP (Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program) position, assisting with seminar/colloquium logistics at Kavli. It indicates she was embedded in the research community but may not have been doing independent astrophysics research.

**Signal value:** An astrophysics background at Kavli gives Mei credibility with technical founders that a typical business/finance background would not. When she evaluates custom ASIC architectures (Etched) or matrix multiplication hardware (MatX), the physics/math foundation matters.
